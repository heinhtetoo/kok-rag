Title: Creamy Coconut Chicken Soup
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Ingredients:
- 500g chicken thigh, diced
- 400ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 1 cup summer squash, sliced
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
Instructions:
Sauté the ginger and chicken thigh in a large pot until brown. Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a simmer. Add the sliced summer squash and cook for 10 minutes until tender. Pour in the coconut milk and fish sauce, reducing the heat to low. Simmer gently for another 5 minutes. Do not boil after adding the coconut milk to prevent splitting. Serve hot.
