Title: Spicy Beef and Squash Stew
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 90 minutes
Ingredients:
- 600g beef chuck, cubed
- 2 tbsp chili paste
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 3 cups beef bone broth
- 2 cups summer squash, cubed
Instructions:
Sear the cubed beef chuck in a heavy pot until a deep crust forms. Remove beef and sauté chopped onions with chili paste until fragrant. Return beef to the pot, pour in beef bone broth, and cover. Simmer on low heat for 75 minutes. Stir in the cubed summer squash and cook uncovered for an additional 15 minutes until the sauce thickens and squash is fork-tender.
